Recipe Haven - Site Plan

Site Name

Recipe Haven

This name communicates a welcoming and reliable place for users to discover, save, and explore delicious recipes. It feels friendly, modern, and clearly conveys the purpose of the site.

Optional domain availability: recipehavenhub.com (example)

Site Purpose

Recipe Haven will serve as a curated collection of recipes with step-by-step instructions, ingredient lists, filtering options, and user-friendly browsing. Users will be able to quickly find recipes by category, explore new meals, and access helpful cooking tips. The site aims to provide an enjoyable and accessible cooking experience for beginners and home chefs.

Scenarios

  • “I need a quick dinner recipe using ingredients I already have. Where can I find simple meal ideas?”
  • “I want to learn a new dessert to impress guests. Where are highly rated or popular recipes?”
  • “I'm new to cooking — can I find easy step-by-step recipes with clear instructions?”

Color Scheme

Selected colors create a warm, clean, appetizing aesthetic suited for a recipe/cooking brand.

  • Primary Color – #C0392B (Tomato Red) Used for headings, buttons, accents.
  • Secondary Color – #F4D03F (Warm Gold) Used for highlights, icons, and callouts.
  • Background – #FFF8F2 (Light Cream) Used for page background to give a cozy kitchen-inspired feel.
  • Text – #333333 (Dark Slate) Used for paragraph text for optimal readability.
